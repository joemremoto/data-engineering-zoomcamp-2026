# Data Engineering Zoomcamp Project Ideas - Analysis

## 1. Philippine Economic Indicator Trends Relative to Neighboring Countries

### Pros
- Rich, structured data available from multiple sources
- High relevance and practical value for policy makers and investors
- Good for time-series analysis and comparative visualizations
- Multiple APIs available (World Bank, IMF, ADB)
- Clear metrics and benchmarks

### Cons
- Data often released with delays (quarterly/annual)
- May require harmonization across different countries' reporting standards
- Some indicators may have gaps or inconsistencies

### Data Source Accessibility: ✅ EXCELLENT
- **World Bank Open Data API** - Comprehensive economic indicators
- **IMF Data API** - Macroeconomic statistics
- **Asian Development Bank (ADB)** - Regional economic data
- **Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA)** - Official PH data
- **ASEAN Stats** - Standardized regional comparisons

---

## 2. PH Students Reading Comprehension

### Pros
- Highly relevant for education policy
- Data exists from international assessments
- Can correlate with socioeconomic factors
- Clear impact measurement potential

### Cons
- Limited public access to granular data
- Data collection is infrequent (every few years)
- May require partnerships with DepEd
- Small sample sizes at regional level

### Data Source Accessibility: ⚠️ MODERATE
- **PISA (Programme for International Student Assessment)** - Limited PH participation
- **SEA-PLM (Southeast Asia Primary Learning Metrics)** - Regional assessment
- **DepEd Reports** - Aggregate data only, may need permission for detailed data
- **World Bank Education Statistics** - High-level indicators

---

## 3. Sickness Trend in PH

### Pros
- High public health relevance
- Can track seasonal patterns and outbreaks
- Rich potential for predictive modeling
- Multiple dimensions (disease type, location, demographics)

### Cons
- Data privacy concerns with health data
- Reporting may be inconsistent across regions
- May require data use agreements
- Underreporting in rural areas

### Data Source Accessibility: ⚠️ MODERATE
- **Department of Health (DOH) DataDrop** - Public health surveillance data
- **WHO Global Health Observatory** - International comparisons
- **Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth)** - Claims data (restricted)
- **Epidemiology Bureau** - Disease surveillance (may require formal request)

---

## 4. Decline in Foreign Investment in PH

### Pros
- Timely and economically significant topic
- Good data availability from government sources
- Can analyze by sector, country of origin
- Clear trends and patterns to explore

### Cons
- May be politically sensitive
- Requires understanding of complex economic factors
- Data definitions may change over time

### Data Source Accessibility: ✅ GOOD
- **Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP)** - FDI statistics
- **Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA)** - Investment data
- **Board of Investments (BOI)** - Sector-specific data
- **ASEAN Investment Report** - Regional context
- **UNCTAD** - International investment database

---

## 5. Trend of Networth of Current Politicians

### Pros
- High public interest and transparency value
- Unique and investigative nature
- Can correlate with positions held over time
- Strong storytelling potential

### Cons
- Data quality and completeness issues
- SALNs (Statement of Assets, Liabilities and Net Worth) not always publicly accessible
- Manual data extraction likely needed
- Legal/ethical considerations
- Difficult to verify accuracy

### Data Source Accessibility: ⚠️ DIFFICULT
- **Office of the Ombudsman** - SALN repository (restricted access)
- **House of Representatives/Senate** - Some available online, inconsistent
- **News archives** - Secondary sources
- **Civil Society Organizations** - Some compiled data (partial)
- **Problem**: Many SALNs are not digitized or publicly available

---

## 6. Campaign Funds vs Politician Salaries

### Pros
- Extremely compelling narrative
- Addresses governance and accountability
- Clear comparative analysis potential
- High social impact

### Cons
- Incomplete campaign finance reporting
- Difficult to access detailed data
- Under-reporting and "off-the-books" spending
- Legal complexity in data interpretation

### Data Source Accessibility: ⚠️ DIFFICULT
- **COMELEC (Commission on Elections)** - Campaign finance reports (limited digital access)
- **Department of Budget and Management (DBM)** - Official salaries (available)
- **Media investigations** - Secondary data sources
- **Problem**: Campaign spending significantly underreported; data often in PDF format

---

## 7. Tourism Indicators

### Pros
- Rich, multi-dimensional data
- Multiple data sources available
- Practical business applications
- Good for time-series and forecasting
- Recovery post-pandemic is timely topic

### Cons
- Data collection may be inconsistent across regions
- Some private sector data may be restricted

### Data Source Accessibility: ✅ EXCELLENT
- **Department of Tourism (DOT)** - Visitor statistics, tourism receipts
- **PSA Tourism Satellite Account** - Economic impact data
- **Manila International Airport Authority (MIAA)** - Passenger data

- **UNWTO** - International benchmarks

---

## 8. Flight Prices

### Pros
- Dynamic, real-time data available
- Great for price prediction models
- Practical consumer application
- Rich feature engineering opportunities

### Cons
- APIs can be expensive for comprehensive data
- Data collection requires continuous scraping
- Historical data may be difficult to obtain
- Rate limiting on APIs

### Data Source Accessibility: ⚠️ MODERATE-EXPENSIVE
- **Skyscanner API** - Limited free tier
- **Amadeus Travel API** - Developer access available
- **Google Flights** - Scraping possible but against ToS
- **Kiwi.com API** - Partners access
- **Web scraping** - Technical and legal considerations

---

## 9. Weather Station Data

### Pros
- Excellent data quality and availability
- Well-structured, continuous time-series data
- Multiple use cases (agriculture, disaster prep)
- Good for IoT/real-time data pipeline practice

### Cons
- May require understanding meteorological concepts
- Data volume can be large for multiple stations
- Historical data gaps in some locations

### Data Source Accessibility: ✅ EXCELLENT
- **PAGASA (Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration)** - Official weather data
- **NOAA Global Historical Climatology Network** - International data
- **OpenWeatherMap API** - Current and historical data
- **Weather Underground API** - Community weather stations
- **Project NOAH** - Geo-hazard data (if still operational)

---

## 10. Public Transport API

### Pros
- High practical utility
- Potential for real-time data pipelines
- Can create valuable public service
- Good for mobile/web app integration

### Cons
- Limited formal APIs in the Philippines
- May require significant data collection/partnerships
- Real-time data infrastructure may not exist
- Inconsistent schedule adherence

### Data Source Accessibility: ⚠️ DIFFICULT
- **LTFRB (Land Transportation Franchising and Regulatory Board)** - Route data (limited digital access)
- **EDSA.ph** - Traffic data (limited)
- **Problem**: No comprehensive public transport API exists
- **Alternative**: Could create one by aggregating multiple sources
- **Jeepney/bus routes** - Mostly crowdsourced/unofficial data

---

## 11. MyWhoosh vs Zwift (Virtual Cycling Platforms)

### Pros
- Niche but growing market
- Tech-savvy audience
- Interesting competitive analysis
- Unique dataset

### Cons

## 13. Condo Supply in the Philippines

### Pros
- Real estate is data-rich sector
- High practical value for investors/buyers
- Clear growth trends to analyze
- Multiple data points (prices, locations, features)

### Cons
- Property data often fragmented
- Private brokers/developers control much data
- May require web scraping multiple sources
- Data standardization challenges

### Data Source Accessibility: ✅ GOOD

- **Santos Knight Frank** - Real estate research
- **Lamudi/Property24** - Listing data (scraping possible)
- **PSA Construction Statistics** - Building permits
- **Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas - Real Estate Exposure** - Lending data
- **Local Government Units** - Building permit data (varies by city)

## Key Factors for Selection

### For Strong Data Engineering Portfolio:
- Choose projects with **API integration** (shows technical skills)
- Include **real-time/streaming data** if possible (Kafka, Spark Streaming)
- Demonstrate **data quality** and **transformation** (DBT, data cleaning)
- Show **orchestration** (Airflow, Prefect, Kestra)
- Include **cloud deployment** (GCP, AWS)
- Create **visualization dashboard** (Looker, Tableau, Streamlit)

### Personal Recommendation:
Consider **Tourism Indicators** or **Weather Station Data** as they:
- Have excellent data availability
- Allow for multiple data sources (batch + streaming)
- Have practical, demonstrable value
- Permit full end-to-end pipeline implementation
- Include opportunities for ML/forecasting
- Are not overly sensitive politically

**Alternative High-Impact Choice:** **Condo Supply** - unique angle, combines web scraping with structured data, high practical value, and demonstrates full data engineering stack.
